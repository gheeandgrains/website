Our Story

Why We Chose Purity
Over Convenience

The Problem We Saw

Mass-produced snacks have sacrificed health for shelf life. Palm oil replaced ghee. Maida replaced millets. Chemicals replaced natural flavors. Our children were growing up on "food-like products" instead of real food.

Our Grandmother's Wisdom

We remembered how our grandmothers made biscuits — with pure ghee, fresh spices, and love. Every ingredient had a purpose. Every recipe was passed down through generations. This wasn't just food; it was nourishment.

Our Mission

We're bringing back real food. Biscuits that your grandmother would approve of. Made with pure desi ghee, ancient millets, and traditional recipes. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just pure tradition, pure taste.
